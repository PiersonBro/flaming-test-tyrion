The dwarf is well, a dwarfish humanoid. They are known for their incredible mining skills and their ability to find beauty in dirt. Unlike many races the dwarves only feel at home in the depths of the earth. The dwarves can be grumpy though it can depend on where their from, just like other races, dwarves have established cultures that differ place to place.
